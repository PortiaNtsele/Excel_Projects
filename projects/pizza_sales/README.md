# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes a pizza shop’s sales data to uncover key revenue trends, customer ordering behavior, and product performance. The goal is to identify insights that can help improve sales strategy and business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze revenue trends over time (daily and monthly)
* Identify top-performing pizza categories
* Understand customer ordering patterns
* Provide actionable recommendations to improve sales

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Data Visualization

---

## 📊 Dataset

The analysis uses four datasets:

* `order_details.csv` — individual pizza orders
* `orders.csv` — order dates and times
* `pizza_types.csv` — pizza categories
* `pizzas.csv` — pizza sizes and prices

These were cleaned and merged into a single dataset for analysis.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Revenue varies significantly by day of the week, with peak sales occurring on weekends.
* Certain pizza categories consistently outperform others, contributing the majority of total revenue.
* Monthly trends show fluctuations that may indicate seasonal demand patterns.
* A small number of products generate a large portion of total sales (Pareto effect).

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Increase staffing and inventory on high-demand days (e.g., weekends).
* Promote top-performing pizza categories through targeted marketing campaigns.
* Investigate underperforming products and consider menu optimization.
* Introduce seasonal promotions to stabilize monthly revenue fluctuations.
